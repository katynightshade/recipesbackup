Spice Coffee

Photo from CommIncense Culture YouTube

Ingredients:

  • Dark roast coffee beans
  • 1/2-inch stick of cinnamon
  • 1 Chai tea bag preferably a chai blend with cardamom

Steps:

  1. Grind a serving size of coffee beans with the cinnamon.
  2. Dump the contents of the chai bag in with the ground coffee and cinnamon.
  3. Brew as directed for whatever brew technique you choose.
