Elven Lembas Bread

Screenshot from GameRant

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup honey
  • 3 whole kumquats (can substitute orange zest and a bit of juice if desired)
  • 2 teaspoons orange flower or rose water
  • 3 ounces chopped almonds or macadamia nuts
  • ¼ cup melted butter
  • 2 ¼ cup flour (or barley flour of you want to be really accurate)
  • ½ teaspoon salt
  • 2 banana leaves
  • Food-safe rope

Steps:

  1. Place the eggs, butter, honey, kumquats, rose or orange flower water, and nuts in a food processor or blender. Blend on high for 2-4 minutes. Add 1 cup of the flour. Blend for a minute or two.
  2. Put mixture into a bowl and add the remaining flour and the salt. Whisk or stir until well blended.
  3. Bake a small amount of dough (about two tablespoons) at a time on a pizzelle or iron about 15 seconds or until lightly brown, for a flat bread like texture. May also be baked at 350 degrees for about 10-15 minutes.
  4. Wrap in leaf, tie, and serve.
